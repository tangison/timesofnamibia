# Times of Namibia — Editorial Context

## Publication Identity
Times of Namibia (TON) is a digital-first news portal operating under the "Broadsheet Digital" philosophy. It combines the editorial authority of a broadsheet newspaper with real-time data intelligence, serving the information needs of Namibia's 2.6 million citizens.

## Mission Statement
"Namibia. Informed. Instantly." — TON delivers authoritative, efficient, and uniquely Namibian information to its audience.

## Editorial Voice
- **Stoic Observer**: Facts over emotion, precision over speed
- **Namibian Voice**: Every story must connect to Namibia and its people
- **Verification First**: All data must be traceable to primary sources
- **No Sensationalism**: Headlines must be factual, not clickbait

## Coverage Priorities
1. **National Governance**: Parliament, legislation, government policy, budget
2. **Regional Hubs**: //Kharas, Oshana, Khomas, Erongo, Otjozondjupa, Kavango East, Zambezi
3. **Economy & Business**: Mining, energy, agriculture, trade, investment
4. **Transaction Services**: Tenders, jobs, market data — direct utility to readers
5. **Legal Desk**: Supreme Court judgments, legislation updates, legal analysis
6. **Politics**: Elections, party platforms, voting records, policy debate
7. **Environment & Climate**: Conservation, drought, desert ecology, water resources
8. **Sports**: Brave Warriors, NPL, athletics, rugby, netball
9. **Culture & Opinion**: Arts, heritage, editorials, letters to the editor
10. **Education**: UNAM, tertiary institutions, scholarships, policy

## Sourcing Rules
- Primary sources (government portals, court websites, official feeds) always preferred
- Secondary sources (news outlets) must be clearly attributed
- Regional click characters must be preserved (e.g., //Kharas)
- Numerical data displayed in JetBrains Mono per brand guidelines
- All scraped timestamps recorded in 6-second convention (Times OS)

## Content Quality Standards
- Articles must have minimum 50 characters of substantive content
- Headlines must be factual, not sensational
- No duplicate articles (deduplicate by URL/GUID)
- Category mapping must follow the 12-category taxonomy
- Reading time estimated at 230 WPM
